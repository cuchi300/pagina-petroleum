Our product range

At Paz Petroleum, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of high-quality petroleum products that cater to a wide spectrum of industrial and commercial applications. Our diverse product offerings include:

Gasoline

Gasoline, available in different octane ratings, is essential for powering automobiles, motorcycles, and small engines. Our gasoline products meet rigorous quality standards, ensuring optimal engine performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control.

Diesel EN590

Diesel EN590, renowned for its low sulfur content and high cetane number, is the standard diesel fuel for modern engines. It ensures optimal engine performance, reduced emissions, and compliance with stringent environmental regulations.

Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG)

LNG, a clean and efficient alternative to traditional fuels, is used in power generation, transportation, and industrial processes. Its low emissions profile, cost-effectiveness, and abundance make it a sustainable choice for energy needs.

Jet Fuel A1

Jet Fuel A1, designed for aviation use, meets strict quality and safety standards set by international aviation authorities. Its purity, stability, and low freezing point make it the preferred choice for airlines, ensuring safe and efficient flights.

D2 (Gasoil)

D2, also known as Gasoil, is a versatile fuel used in various industries, including transportation, manufacturing, and agriculture. With its low sulfur content and high energy density, D2 offers superior performance and environmental benefits.

Bitumen 60/70

Bitumen 60/70 is a versatile and durable asphalt binder widely used in road construction, waterproofing, and industrial applications. It offers excellent adhesion, weather resistance, and longevity, making it a preferred choice for infrastructure projects worldwide.

Crude Oil

Crude Oil, the primary raw material for petroleum products, undergoes rigorous testing and quality control measures. Our partnerships with reputable producers ensure that we deliver crude oil of the highest quality and purity.

Petroleum Coke

Petroleum Coke, a byproduct of refining processes, serves as a valuable resource in various industries. It is used in fuel blending, metallurgical processes, and as a source of carbon for manufacturing purposes.

We prioritize quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction in every product we offer. Our partnerships with leading refineries and adherence to international standards guarantee that you receive premium-grade petroleum products tailored to your specific requirements.
